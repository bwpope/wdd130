Epic Runs

Test yourself against these epic runs! These three runs will test your ability to climb and go the distance!

Herbert Half

Run to the ghost town of Herbert Idaho while enjoying the rolling hills of the dry farms. This route starts at University and Millhollow and goes 14 miles to the end of the pavement, to make it a full marathon just run back!

Menan Butte

This iconic run may not be long but it will have your legs and butt burrning in no time!. Take the north trailhead until it connects with the service road. Then run counter clockwise around the caldera. Bring good shoes and water!

The Beast

This route is 12 miles of up and down, including the iconic Sommers Hill aptly called "The Beast." This route also includes "The Hardest Part" secgment that will make you wonder if this run will ever end!
